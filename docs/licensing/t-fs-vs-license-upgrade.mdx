# Provisioning a License to a Device

Use the Device Licensing tab in Aurora Focus to provision licenses to devices remotely.

Provisioning licenses to devices without internet access while the devices are connected to Aurora Focus.

1. Click ![](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-device-menu-ico.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg) and select

Provision from the menu.

![](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-licensing-provisioning-1.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

2. Select the device to provision the license to and click Done.

![](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-licensing-provision-device.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

3. Observe the Device Successfully Provisioned notification.

![](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-licensing-provisioning-success.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)
