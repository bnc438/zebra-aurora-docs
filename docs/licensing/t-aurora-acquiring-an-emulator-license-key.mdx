Use Aurora Focus to obtain an emulator license key.

1. Click **Help** from the top navigation menu in Aurora Focus.

2. Select **License** from the drop-down menu.

![aurora focus help menu](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-mint-help-license.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

3. Observe the **License** Activate popup and click the link:

![license activation popup ](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-mint-license-activate.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

4. Enter your email address. This becomes your User ID.

![email registration for emulator license](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-focus-mint-emulator-1.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

5. Click **Next**.

6. Verify your email address by entering the verification code sent to your inbox.

![emulator license verification code](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-focus-mint-emulator-2.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

7. Click **Next**.

8. Enter your name and password, then select your country from the drop-down menu.

![emulator license acquisition enter user information](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-focus-mint-emulator-3.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

9. Click **Submit and Continue**.

10. Enter your user and company information and provide the number of licenses you are requesting.

![emulator license information enter license quantity](https://www.zebra.com/content/dam/techpubs/media/machine-vision/aurora-focus/g-aurora-focus-mint-emulator-4.png/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

11. Click **Submit**.

12. Enter the **Activation ID** into the **License Key** field after registration to activate the license.
