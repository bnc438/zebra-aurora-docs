Use the Jobs tab in Device Settings to manage job slots, rename, or delete jobs.
Go to
Device Details
Select
Jobs
from the menu.
Startup - indicates the job that runs upon the camera's startup.
Active - indicates a job that is in edit mode.
Available - indicates a job that is deployed to the device but not running.
Click filter by slot, name, job type, last updated, size, or status.
Click
Edit
to edit the job in build mode.
Click
to set the job as running, clear the running jobs, set the selected job as the Startup job, clear the Startup job, delete, duplicate, or rename a job.
Click
and click
Swap Job
to swap the current job slot with another available slot.
Select the job slot to swap the current job with.
Click
Swap Job
to confirm the change.
