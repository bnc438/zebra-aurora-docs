Configuration and deployment of FIS or MV jobs are facilitated using the Capture, Build, and Connect tabs.

-
-
-
Capture
- configure the Job Mode and Source settings on the Triggers tab and imager settings on the Acquisition Settings tab.
Build
- depending on the toolset (FIS or MV), configure the settings and symbologies or tools used to complete a specific job.
Connect
- save and configure image settings and network connections such as Industrial Ethernet, Output Formatting, and GPIO Mapping.
