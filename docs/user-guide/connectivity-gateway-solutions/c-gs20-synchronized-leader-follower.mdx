In synchronized leader-follower use cases, the leader receives a trigger, activates the follower devices, receives the data from the followers, and sends the results to the host.

Examples:
- Multi-sided reading of parcels.
- Multi-point reading of barcodes on a tire rim (overhead view only).

**Leader Input:**

- Generic: GPIO Trigger
- From Follower: Results

**Leader Output:**

- To Host: TCPIP, Industrial Communication, Serial, USB HID
- To Follower: Trigger
- To Machine/PLC: Total Result String, Total Pass/Fail GPIO

**Functionalities:**

- Enable synchronous triggers from leader to follower devices.
- Receive results from follower devices and apply pass/fail criteria.
- Send result data to the host, GPIO, or PLC.
