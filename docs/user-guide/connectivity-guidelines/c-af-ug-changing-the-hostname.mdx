To change the hostname on the device:


1. Locate the hostname field.
2. Enter the desired hostname:
3. Click
Locate the hostname field.
Enter the desired hostname:
The hostname must be alphanumeric and have a maximum length of 26 characters.
Click
Apply
when complete.
The device, application, and network hardware may need to be power-cycled for the hostname change to occur.
The new hostname is visible following the device reboot.
